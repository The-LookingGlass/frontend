The Looking Glass
Project

The objective of this project is to build a geographical heatmap visualization of jobs and basic resources needed in a day-to-day life (focussing across Ireland for now).We anticipate this project will help address the social issue of over-urbanization and the decline of rural Ireland by: 1. Assisting people in smaller towns and rural Ireland find jobs close to their location - in visual format. 2. Helping returning emigrants to identify jobs outside of major cities; as well as available housing in the vicinity.

Our solution: A visualized HeatMap of Ireland to allow users to filter for jobs and houses and provide context-based information to the visitors of the site
